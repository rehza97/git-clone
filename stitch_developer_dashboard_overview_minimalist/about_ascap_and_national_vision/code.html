Preserving Our Intellectual Heritage

In an era where software defines the capabilities of nations, the preservation of source code is not merely a technical task—it is a matter of national security and cultural heritage. ASCAP was founded with a singular vision: to create a resilient, sovereign digital infrastructure.

Our platform serves as the definitive bridge between the academic sector and the industrial startup ecosystem. By archiving the output of thousands of university projects, we prevent the loss of innovation and facilitate the transfer of technology as mandated by Decree 1275.

"Digital sovereignty is not an option, but a necessity for the modern state. ASCAP is our answer to the challenge of data independence."
